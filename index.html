About Me

Hi, I am Md. Arafat Jamil, a professional in Building Engineering and Construction Management, specializing in construction safety, process automation, and the integration of advanced technologies such as BIM and the IoT. With a B.Sc. from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), I leverage data-driven strategies, intelligent monitoring, and predictive analytics to enhance construction efficiency, safety, and sustainability. My work bridges research and practice, focusing on developing smart, resilient, and sustainable building solutions aligned with global standards.

In addition to construction engineering & management, I have strong expertise in structural engineering, including the design, analysis, and optimization of steel, composite, and reinforced concrete structures. As a Design Engineer, I apply finite element modeling, seismic hazard assessment, and performance-based design to deliver safe and sustainable outcomes. My research explores structural performance optimization, seismic resilience, sustainable materials, and IoT-enabled SHM, merging digital innovation with engineering excellence.
